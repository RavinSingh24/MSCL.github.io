Ravin Singh

Founder and CEO

Having founded the Competitive Coding Club (CCC) at my local school, I initiated weekly programming competitions for 7th and 8th graders, fostering an environment of learning and healthy competition. Reflecting on the success of the CCC, I recognized the potential to extend these opportunities to a broader community of students.

Simultaneously, my involvement in the Technology Student Association (TSA) on a national level provided me with insights into the importance of collaboration and skill development in the realm of technology. It was during this time of reflection and engagement that the idea for the Middle School Coding League (MSCL) was conceived.

As an active scout, I've honed leadership skills through leading patrols and organizing community projects. Simultaneously, I've explored various programming languages, deeply engaging with technology. This dual experience has equipped me with versatile skills and a passion for innovation.

Jatin Sao

COO

Together with a trusted colleague, I co-founded the CCC (Competitive Coding Club), driven by our shared ambition to transcend the boundaries of our educational institution. This ambition materialized into the establishment of the MSCL (Middle School Coding League), a testament to our dedication to fostering excellence beyond our immediate environment.

My foray into coding was sparked by a passion for video games, leading me to explore the intricacies of virtual landscapes. I invite you to join me in discovering the limitless possibilities of technology, where each line of code holds the potential for extraordinary creations.
